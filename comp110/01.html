This session introduces the notion of the "computer", through the lens of its historical development from antiquity to present day. This lays the foundation for your subsequent studies of computing.

Learning Objectives:
  • Recall the historical context of computing and gaming technology
  • Explain the basic architecture of a computer
  • Distinguish the most common programming languages and paradigms in use today
